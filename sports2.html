Football and Basketball:
Bolden’s Historic Win in 2023 FIFA Woman’s World Cup in New ZealandBy Roxan D. Resuello
Filipinos love their sports, they love their players and they love playing it.Basketball has been a prominent sport in the Philippines, dating back to when the Americans had snatched the ownership of the country from the hands of the Spaniards, it is meant to be a lasting impression of the colonialism that happened in the country. Basketball is not really associated with the dark side of history rather it is associated with the interesting and easy mechanics it brought. Even now, Basketball is still the most popular sport in the Philippines.

Out of nowhere, and just recently, The Filipinas, a Filipino Football team, won the 2023 FIFA Women’s World Cup last July 25, 2023. The one who is behind the historic goal was Sarina Bolden, a half-Pangasinense and African American descent.

For 90 minutes of holding down their defense, Bolden assured their win when the goalkeeper lost her grip on the ball and Bolden made that historic score against New Zealand’s Football Fern.

This win, however, stirred a thought about whether football does fit Filipinos instead of Basketball.Infrastructure and EquipmentFor Basketball, people even use make shift basketball rings and an overused ball. On every street, there would be a ring that signifies the Filipinos' love for basketball even if all the equipments are makeshift. Even Sangguniang Kabataan will propose a program of Basketball Liga where teenagers from their barangay can play and compete in Basketball. To make sports popular, a basic infrastructure is needed for it to be even playable, that is why Basketball is famous because the government can even fund the instruction and equipment.

For Football, it can be a bit expensive for that matter, a wide field is needed to play but you only need a ball to kick. Rice fields that were freshly trimmed off the harvest could even be a playing field. No need for a ring that would cost much to maintain.Mechanics and RulesIn Football, the only thing a player should keep in their mind is do not let the ball out of their sight (or foot), do not let other players snatch it away, and do not hit them either then run towards their goal. In Basketball, a player will also not let the ball out of their sight but keep sight of their own teammate and the other team so that they could make a pass towardsb their player because if the ball lasts for long in their hand. Players need to be quick and have fast decision making skills so they do not gain any fouls for handling the ball wrong.Advantages and disadvantagesIn Basketball, being taller is a great advantage, that is why a lot of players will be roughly 200cm (6’7) tall. The average height of a person in the Philippines has the average height as roughly 164cm (5’5), unless the player is witty enough to get the other’s hand away from the ball, which is impossible proximity. Going against a 200cm tall player, will probably not gain you an advantage. In Football, height does not matter as long as they are quick on their feet and can run away from the players who will snatch away the ball.Famous Filipino PlayersRecently, Sarina Bolden made a historic mark in Football. Winning the 2023 FIFA Women’s World Cup in New Zealand. Even though on July 30, 2023, they lost against Norway with a points of 6-0, their win in New Zealand still left a lasting impression on the history of Football in the country.

Bolden is definitely a significant player of Filipino descent, while in Basketball, sometimes people lose count of famous players who are winning and gaining recognition in International competitions.For fun times and memoriesIt's very important to note that people will treat different sports differently, someone will think that Football is easy while still thinking that Basketball cannot be changed as one of the famous sports in the Philippines and someone will think that Football should have been the sports of the country because of how overrated Basketball is.

Filipinos love their sports, they love their players and they love playing it. They will never change their mind over the feelings and experiences they get whenever they play Basketball, it has been a long tradition and past timenfor Filipinos that it does not matter if Filipinos can be good at other sports. There are still significant Filipinos who will strive to be the best players in their chosen sports and people should still remind themselves, that it’s fine that there are differences in their love for sports, as long as they enjoy it, people will experience fun times and create memories.
