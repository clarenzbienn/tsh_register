PHINMA-UPang Students Shine in IECEP Quiz Show and Math Olympiad
By Mark Daniel L. BarcelonaOctober 7, 2023
Trained to solve. PHINMA-UPang students recieved certificates among collaborating colleges at Virgen Milagrosa University Foundation on October 7, 2023.
Students from PHINMA- University of Pangasinan (PHINMA-UPang) proved their instructional excellence in the 8th Interscholastic Quiz Show and 2nd Math Olympiad prepared by way of the Institute of Electronics Engineers of the Philippines (IECEP) on October 7, 2023 at the Virgen Milagrosa University Foundation.The IECEP Quiz Show and Math Olympiad is an annual event that goals to sell educational excellence and camaraderie among electronics engineering college students in the region. It additionally serves as a venue for college students to showcase their competencies and potentials inside the subject of electronics engineering and mathematics.
