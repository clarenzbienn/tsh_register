Ex-CJ Sereno holds good governance seminar at PHINMA-UPangBy Isshael EspirituSeptember 27, 2023DAGUPAN CITY — Former Chief Justice Maria Lourdes Sereno paid a visit to PHINMA-University of Pangasinan and offered a seminar on September 27, 2023 as part of the Siyak Ya Mismo seminar series.Sereno tackled national issues and laws about sovereignty. Good governance was also given emphasis. An open fora with representatives was also done — people representing various sectors such as the PHINMA-UPang faculty took part in asking questions about the discussion.

PHINMA-UPang College of Urdaneta (PUCU) has also participated in the seminar.

Learning materials were provided at the end of the session through a QR code on the same scope as Sereno discussed – religion, laws, good governance, and so on.
