PHINMA University of Pangasinan Leads Brigada Eskwela 2023 with a Commitment to Community WelfareBy Quency Gylle C. CabralAugust, 2023
Service as commitment. PHINMA-UPang leads Brigada Eskwela 2023 from August 22-23, 2023.
In a resounding testament to their decade-long commitment to community service, PHINMA University of Pangasinan is set to lead the Brigada Eskwela 2023, continuing their mission to make lives better beyond the classroom walls. The university, renowned for its educational excellence, has been actively involved in uplifting communities, aiding students, teachers, and contributing to environmental conservation.The IECEP Quiz Show and Math Olympiad is an annual event that goals to sell educational excellence and camaraderie among electronics engineering college students in the region. It additionally serves as a venue for college students to showcase their competencies and potentials inside the subject of electronics engineering and mathematics.
