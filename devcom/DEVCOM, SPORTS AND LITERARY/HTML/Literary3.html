I AM, I WILL BE
By Raeve
I am Wendy. My looks don’t define me. I’m not a painting to leer at, nor am I a doll for others to decide how I dress up. I don’t need to prove myself to the society. I will never change the way I am. Whether I’m pretty or not, it will never affect my capabilities. I am my own person. I will be who I wanted to be.

I am Odette. My gender doesn’t define me. I am capable just like others do. I can work just as much as they do. I don’t care about others pushing me down, because I can still rise up. I’m not weak as others perceive me to be. I am a hard worker. I will be the boss of my own destiny.

I am Mary. My past will never bind me. I was a victim but I survived and is still living. I suffered, but that will not stop me from fighting. I won’t let those nightmares shackle me. I have shed enough tears, and it made me as strong as I can be. I am a survivor. I will be the voice of those in need.

I am Annie. My choices will never define me. I am a mother and a father of my own children. I will not be a martyr to tie myself in a relationship that will threaten my children’s security. I will bare my fangs for them. I am a mother. I will be the head and light of my family.

I am Nadie. Nothing can hinder me. I am free to do what all humans do. I am free to ask and speak. I am free to learn anything and everything. I can decide for myself for I am capable to be what a human can be. The sky’s the limit for whatever I can be. I am a woman. I will be a significant part of the history.
In the promise of a brighter, equal tomorrow,
For a world where love, respect, and equity follow.
