EXPECT
By Louis
He expects a housewife
But can’t provide a life
She is walking in a knife
But everybody is in strife

Oh, is this how it is supposed to be?
An equality that chooses; not me
A world who favors a bee,
But never the woman who gives the tree

Time says it will heal you
Yet society will never be new
A cruel reality, I don’t want to view
Change is something only done by a few

It might take million stones
Who knows where it will go?
She has the right to grow
In a society that doesn’t glow

She expects a life
A man can never be a wife
He is walking in a knife
The society will always be in strife
