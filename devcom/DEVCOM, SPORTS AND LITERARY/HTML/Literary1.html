DO YOU HEAR ME?By QuenchivesDo you hear me?
In the whispers of the past,
Where dreams of equality were steadfast?
In the shadows of history, a silent plea,
For a world where justice reigns, for you and me.

Do you hear me?
In the battles we've fought,
In the struggles for equal ground sought?
In the strength of women, rising above,
For a world where every heart find love.

Do you hear me?
In the voices that unite,
Championing a world where all have a right?
In the quest for balance, in every plea,
For a future where we all are free.

Do you hear me?
In the stories we share,
Of women's resilience, their unyielding care?
In the chapters unwritten, the pages yet to be,
A tapestry of hope, of equality.

Do you hear me?
In the colors we display,
In the vibrant spectrum of life, come what may?
In the diversity of souls that we see,
A world where differences make us whole, you and me.

Do you hear me?
In the rhythm of change,
A melody of progress, rearranged?
In the harmony we seek, a symphony,
For a world where justice and equality shall be.

Do you hear me?
In these words, sincere and true,
A call for a world where dreams can come through?
In the promise of a brighter, equal tomorrow,
For a world where love, respect, and equity follow.
THE OFFICIAL PUBLICATION OF PHINMA UNIVERSITY OF PANGASINAN
