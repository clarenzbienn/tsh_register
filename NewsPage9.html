Diokno gave a discussion on good governance which was followed by a panel discussion with panelists of various sectors. Picture taking sessions were held afterwards. College of Education and Liberal Arts (CELA) Dean Cherry Calaunan represented the faculty, Association of Political Science Students (APSS) Vice President Marc Avyn Santos stood for the student leaders, Mirheal de Vera of the youth ministry of (parish), and Malued Barangay Captain Filipina delos Santos spoke for the barangay officials.

The event was organized by the Center for Student Development and Leadership (CSDL) and Dagupan People’s Council. Participants included around 500 people from various college departments of the main campus and PHINMA-UPang College of Urdaneta (PUCU) as well as the Integrated Bar of the Philippines Dagupan (IBP).
