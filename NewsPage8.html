PHINMA-University of Pangasinan teams win awards in 2023 IECEP LUPANG SC Undergraduate Research CompetitionBy Mark Daniel L. Barcelona October 3, 2023Two teams of undergraduate students from the PHINMA-University of Pangasinan have won prestigious awards in the 2023 IEECP LUPANG SC Undergraduate Research Competition, a regional event that showcases innovative projects in the fields of science and engineering.The first team, named SMART-RAFT, won the Best Capstone Award under the Mechatronics and Robotics category for their project that aims to develop a smart and autonomous floating aquaculture aid. The SMART-RAFT is an aquaculture device that has three main functions: aeration, feeding, and tracking. The device can monitor the water quality, feed the fish, and track what are the volumes of feeds that is being fed. The team consists of Lenald D. Pacleb as the lead researcher and also the presente , and the members are Patrick Jaus P. Catabay, Angelica Mae B. Obcena, and Junnel D. Soriano, who are all students of the Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering program.

The second team, named iCARE, won the Best Paper Award under the internet of things category for their paper that proposes a novel system for monitoring basic health assessments using wireless sensors and cloud computing. The iCARE is a healthcare assistant device with three major features: WiOX for oximeter reading, WiSeer for UV light disinfection, and WiNotif for notifications. The device can measure the oxygen saturation level, disinfect the room using UV light, and alert the user or the caregiver using a mobile app. The team consists of Jasper R. Barbozan as the team leader, Guerlaine Faye Q. Nitas as the presenter, and the members are Kassandra Mae L. Finuliar, Kenneth Jeremie C. Ferrer, and Junel B. Prado, who are also students of the Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering program.

Both teams were coached by Engr. Fermar Fajardo, a faculty member of the College of Engineering and Architecture at the PHINMA-University of Pangasinan.
The 2023 IEECP LUPANG SC Undergraduate Research Competition was organized by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers - Philippines Section (IEEE-PH) and the LUPANG SC Foundation, a non-government organization that promotes science and technology education in the country. The competition was held in Lyceum-Northwestern University on October 1, 2023.

Both teams are going to advance in the National Finals with three stages which are the screening, semi-finals, and finals round. The screening and semi-finals round will be held online while the Finals round will be held in face-to-face set-up in Marikina City on October 26, 2023
As part of their awards, both teams will also have the opportunity to present their projects at the International Conference of Pangasinan and Bolosan Studies, which will be held in Lingayen on November 20-22, 2023. The conference is an annual event that features multidisciplinary research on various aspects of Pangasinan and Bolosan culture, history, language, literature, arts, and society.
