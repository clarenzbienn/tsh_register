OUR COMPANY
The Students’ Herald, having been founded in 1988, is a duly recognized institution in PHINMA-University of Pangasinan and carry the advocacy line “Truth Above All.” The organization serves as the channel for information dissemination, transparency, and freedom of expression and acts as an autonomous institution, performing duties to uphold, defend, and protect the rights of students.
MISSION:The Students' Herald shall carry the advocacy line “Truth Above All” and will be committed to the journalistic integrity of preserving its right to inform students, regardless of any form of school administration and student government intimidations, that set a boundary of not violating any moral or legal obligations in keeping the line that upholds, defends, and protects the students’ rights to information dissemination transparency, and freedom of expression all the while as an autonomous institution, will not go above any moral and ethical standards and laws of the land.
VISION:The Students' Herald, as an autonomous institution, shall inscribe in its vision to set transparencies of a functional truth and will competently publish reliable reports and articles to further the objective of journalistic standards above self-interest or assumptions and to preserve the rights of the students of PHINMA-University of Pangasinan to a fair distribution of information from within and outside of the university premises and to act as the channel of students’ reactions to school policies, grievances and complaints that need immediate action.
